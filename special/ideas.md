# PressCraft — Design Brainstorm

## Approach A — Editorial Precision
**Design Movement:** Swiss International Typographic Style meets luxury editorial
**Core Principles:** Rigid grid discipline, typographic hierarchy as architecture, ink-on-paper tactility, negative space as a luxury signal
**Color Philosophy:** Pure white (#FFFFFF) as the canvas of the press, deep black (#0A0A0A) as the authority of ink, gold (#C9A646) as the hallmark of craft — used sparingly to mark excellence
**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric editorial grid with full-bleed image columns; text blocks offset against imagery; section dividers use thin gold hairlines
**Signature Elements:** Oversized serif display numerals for section labels; thin gold rule lines; full-bleed black-and-white photography with gold color-overlay on hover
**Interaction Philosophy:** Restrained elegance — hover reveals gold underlines, images desaturate on load and gain color on hover, scroll triggers slow reveal
**Animation:** Fade-up on scroll (60ms stagger), gold underline slides in from left on hover, hero text splits word-by-word
**Typography System:** Playfair Display (headings, display) + DM Sans (body, UI) — high contrast serif/sans pairing

## Approach B — Atelier Noir
**Design Movement:** Parisian luxury atelier — haute couture applied to print
**Core Principles:** Monochromatic restraint, gold as punctuation not decoration, architectural whitespace, materiality through texture
**Color Philosophy:** White as premium paper stock, black as the weight of authority, gold as the embossed seal — never used in large areas, always as a fine detail
**Layout Paradigm:** Full-viewport sections with deliberate asymmetry; hero uses a split-screen (text left, image right); services use a large-number index system
**Signature Elements:** Thin-weight serif numerals as section indices; embossed-look gold borders; paper-texture overlays on dark sections
**Interaction Philosophy:** Everything moves with intention — no gratuitous animation, only purposeful transitions that reinforce the premium feel
**Animation:** Clip-path reveal on scroll (curtain wipe), gold border draws on hover, hero headline staggered letter-by-letter
**Typography System:** Cormorant Garamond (display, headings) + Jost (body, navigation) — ultra-high contrast pairing
<probability>0.07</probability>

## Approach C — Industrial Craft
**Design Movement:** Contemporary craft meets industrial printing heritage
**Core Principles:** Raw material honesty, precision engineering aesthetic, craft-as-luxury, bold typographic statements
**Color Philosophy:** White as the blank page of possibility, near-black (#111111) as the weight of the press, gold as the gilded edge of a premium book — used as accent only on key moments
**Layout Paradigm:** Horizontal scroll gallery for portfolio; staggered masonry for services; full-bleed hero with text overlay using large tracking
**Signature Elements:** Wide-tracked uppercase labels; mechanical grid overlays; press-mark style monogram logo
**Interaction Philosophy:** Tactile and satisfying — hover states feel like pressing a key, transitions feel mechanical yet smooth
**Animation:** Scale-up on hover (1.02), staggered grid reveal on scroll, hero uses a typewriter-style reveal for the tagline
**Typography System:** Libre Baskerville (display) + Inter (body) — classic book serif with modern sans

## Selected Approach: **B — Atelier Noir**

Cormorant Garamond for headings (ultra-refined, high-contrast serif) + Jost for body/UI (geometric, clean). Full-viewport asymmetric sections, gold as fine punctuation, clip-path scroll reveals, split-screen hero, large-numeral section indices.
